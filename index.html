What Happens During a Facial Treatment?

During a facial downtown Toronto, the treatment typically begins with an in-depth consultation. Your esthetician will analyze your skin and discuss your skincare goals. The process then usually starts with a deep cleanse to remove dirt, oil, and makeup. After cleansing, exfoliation is done to slough off dead skin cells, followed by extraction to clear clogged pores and address any blemishes.

Once the skin is prepped, a treatment mask suited to your skin type will be applied. For example, if you have dry skin, the mask might be packed with hydrating ingredients like hyaluronic acid. If your skin is acne-prone, the mask might contain purifying clay or salicylic acid to help clear pores. The final step of most facials is a gentle facial massage to stimulate blood flow and lymphatic drainage, leaving your skin refreshed and glowing.
